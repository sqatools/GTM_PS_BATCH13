HEALTH INSURANCE POLICY DOCUMENT

1. WAITING PERIODS
The waiting period for health insurance is defined as follows:
- Standard waiting period: 30 days from the date of policy issuance
- For emergency medical conditions: No waiting period applies
- For planned surgeries: 90 days waiting period
- Pre-existing conditions: 12 months waiting period

2. COVERAGE DETAILS
Our health insurance covers:
- Hospitalization expenses up to $100,000 per year
- Out-patient treatment up to $10,000 per year
- Dental treatment up to $5,000 per year
- Vision care up to $2,000 per year
- Mental health services up to $15,000 per year

3. CLAIM SETTLEMENT
- Claims must be filed within 30 days of discharge
- Maximum claim processing time: 15 working days
- Documentation required: Medical receipts, discharge summary, and prescription details
- Claim denial reasons: Non-compliance with policy terms, fraudulent claims, services not covered

4. EXCLUSIONS
The following items are NOT covered under this policy:
- Cosmetic procedures
- Experimental treatments
- Sports injuries
- Self-inflicted injuries
- Alcohol or drug-related treatment
- Treatment abroad (unless pre-approved)

5. PREMIUM AND PAYMENT
- Annual premium: Based on age and health status
- Payment frequency options: Monthly, Quarterly, Semi-annual, Annual
- Late payment penalty: 10% of monthly premium
- Grace period for payment: 30 days

6. NETWORK HOSPITALS
Our policy covers treatment at over 5,000 network hospitals across the country.
Cashless facilities available at all network hospitals.

7. POLICY RENEWAL
- Policy renewal date: Annually on policy anniversary
- No re-evaluation of health status required for renewal
- Renewal premium may increase based on claims history and age
- Non-renewal notice period: 60 days
